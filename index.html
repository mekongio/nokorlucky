Welcome to Nokor Lucky - Apartment Hotel

The best-serviced apartment Hotel in Phnom Penh. Conveniently located in the heart of downtown Phnom Penh and only a few minutes walk from many markets and banks, Nokor Lucky is the ideal place to live. Overlooking Boeung Kak Lake, the Royal Palace, and Phnom Penh's skyline, our rooftop offers breathtaking views as you indulge the fresh breeze.

Our friendly and attentive staff will provide you with the best service that you will feel right at home.  With 24 hours of full security service, our apartment is a safe retreat from the bustling streets.

Enjoy the privacy and comfort of your own space in one of our rooms ranging from standard, to deluxe, to suite.  

Each room is fully furnished with high quality furniture and decorated with a blend of modern and traditional Cambodian style.  Stay connected with family or the world through our high speed Internet access.
